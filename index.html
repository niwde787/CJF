The Story Behind LiveSnaps

LiveSnaps was born from a parent's simple question on the sidelines: "What's really happening out there?" I was frustrated by the lack of context during games—trying to understand the strategy, track my child's participation time, and make sense of the action on the field. I knew other parents felt the same, and I suspected even coaches wished for a clearer, more immediate picture of the game.

I envisioned a tool that could bridge this gap for everyone. An app that would empower parents with real insights into their child's performance and, at the same time, equip coaches with powerful data to manage playing time effectively and make smarter strategic decisions. After months of development, LiveSnaps was created. It's the tool I always wished I had in the stands—built to give parents and coaches a shared, powerful new vision of the game.

From sideline data to winning strategy. Most coaches spent countless hours manually tracking plays, compiling stats on clunky spreadsheets, and struggling to give players the specific, data-backed feedback they needed to improve. I knew there had to be a better way.

I envisioned a tool that was as intuitive as drawing on a whiteboard but as powerful as a professional analytics suite. An app that could not only log what happened on the field but could also provide intelligent insights to answer the "why." After months of development, fueled by late nights, LiveSnaps was created get ready to turn sideline data into a winning strategy.
